TANGO

An online Tango management system is a web application. This system was developed to automate the day-to-day activities of a restaurateur. From the website, customers can easily order food from any place and also book their table, position, waiter, etc for lunch or dinner. And from the admin panel, you can monitor everything.

  • Main features
      • Display a food and drink menu on any mobile device with a variable screen size, e.g., tablets and smartphones.
      The food and drink menu can contain pictures.
      There is an Admin panel for restaurant administrators, where they can create, read, update, and delete menu items.
      The restaurant admin will be able to create new users and be able to assign roles such as waiter and admin.
      Identify the customers by location before they can retrieve the food and drink menu. This helps to identify whether the customer is accessing the food and drink menu from the restaurant or not.
      A customer can order one or more food and drink items.
      Send ‘order acceptance’ notification to the customer after a waiter accepts the order.
      In the case of N number of waiters listening for orders from the customer, if a waiter W1 accepts the order O1, the other waiter W2 should not have any access or control over O1.
      Seat switching: customers may switch or change the table from one place to another. In this case, the system needs to support transferring the orders from one place to another. This can be done with the assistance of the waiter.
      The waiter can add/update/delete the orders manually in the system-generated orders.
      Customers can cancel or edit the order before submitting it to the queue.
      The table will show "Booked" until the bill is paid by the customer.
      Once the order is finalized by the customer and set to the queue, the kitchen staff/cook/chef can see that and send an acknowledgement of the order's receipt there.
      A customer can pay via cash or card.
